The Impact of Vehicles on Air Quality

Cars are one of the primary sources of air pollution in urban areas. Emissions from vehicles contribute to a variety of environmental issues, including:

  • Smog
  • Acid rain
  • Respiratory problems in humans

One of the main culprits behind these emissions is a faulty air filter. When air filters are not functioning correctly, they allow dirt and debris to enter the engine, causing it to work harder and produce more harmful emissions.
